# Fun Shui Requirements: Social Furniture Rearrangement Experience

**Date:** May 19, 2026
**Status:** Draft / Discovery
**Project:** Fun Shui (Asheville, NC)

## 1. Executive Summary
Fun Shui is a "ladies night out" social experience that gamifies furniture rearrangement. Positioned as an alternative to "Sip and Paint" or "Escape Rooms," it targets bachelorette parties, tourist groups, and corporate team building in Asheville. The core value is social connection through creative play with vintage/thrifted inventory, supported by wine sales and digital takeaways.

## 2. Core Experience
*   **Tactile Play:** Participants work in groups to arrange "rooms" using a vast inventory of vintage and thrifted furniture.
*   **Format:** Flexible space handling multiple parallel rooms. Supports "Room-vs-Room" competition for larger groups or disparate sessions for smaller pods.
*   **Atmosphere:** A boutique wine bar environment where the experience is the main event.
*   **Digital Artifacts:**
    *   **3D Model:** Participants receive a digital 3D model of their finished room design.
    *   **Highlight Reel (Stretch):** AI-generated video highlights of the session (auto-edited from floor recordings).

## 3. Business Model & Revenue
*   **Primary Revenue:** Experience ticket sales.
*   **Secondary Revenue:** On-site wine sales (boutique bar context).
*   **Inventory Strategy:** Locally sourced Asheville vintage/thrift items to maintain a unique, "Asheville-vibe" aesthetic.
*   **Retail:** Small boutique retail component integrated into the experience space.

## 4. Target Personas
1.  **Celebration Groups:** Bachelorette parties and tourists looking for a unique, Instagrammable Asheville group activity.
2.  **Corporate Team Building:** HR-led groups looking for a collaborative, low-stress creative outlet.

## 5. Success Criteria
*   **Social Engagement:** Measured by group bookings and repeat visits.
*   **Instagrammability:** Frequency of social sharing of digital 3D models and (eventually) highlight reels.
*   **Operational Flow:** Ability to reset rooms quickly between sessions.

## 6. Scope Boundaries
*   **In-Scope:** Physical space requirements, 3D capture/modeling logic, wine bar integration, business model presentation.
*   **Out-of-Scope (for now):** Professional interior design services, high-end new furniture sales, standalone bar operations (bar exists only to support the experience).
