The Concept

A "ladies night out" furniture arrangement business. A tactile, social alternative to Sip & Paint and Escape Rooms, leveraging Asheville's unique vintage aesthetic.

Target Audience

Who It's For

  • Bachelorette Parties: The ultimate Instagrammable group activity.
  • Asheville Tourists: Seeking a "uniquely Asheville" evening.
  • Corporate Teams: Low-stress, high-collaboration team building.
  • Local Friends: A fresh alternative to the standard bar scene.
Revenue Streams

Business Model

  • Experience Tickets: Core per-person event pricing.
  • Boutique Wine Bar: Integrated wine sales during the session.
  • Retail Corner: Small-scale boutique shopping within the space.
The Experience

Core Mechanics

  • Multiple Rooms: Parallel sessions or Room-vs-Room competition.
  • Local Inventory: Vast selection of Asheville vintage & thrifted pieces.
  • Facilitated Fun: High-energy hosts to guide the "play."
Digital Artifacts

The Takeaway

  • 3D Model: A digital twin of the group's finished room design.
  • AI Highlight Reel: (Stretch) Automated video of the night's best moments.
  • Social Shareables: Ready-to-post content for every participant.
